What Exactly Is Machine Learning?

Let's break it down. Think of Machine Learning as teaching a computer to make decisions based on patterns. For example:

  • A model is like a recipe that guides the computer on what to do.
  • The data is the ingredients‚Äîit's what the computer "learns" from.
  • The process of training the model is like practicing cooking the dish repeatedly until it tastes just right.

So, if Netflix is recommending movies, it's because its model (recipe) has learned from billions of viewing patterns (ingredients) to figure out what you might enjoy next.
